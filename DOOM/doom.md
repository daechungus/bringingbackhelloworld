### Classic DOOM

As torturous as this was to me when I had to finish this project, I look back at it quite fondly. It was a fun experience building all night long with my fellow classmates while we struggled with memory management and path optimization. 

Instructions:

I to open inventory
W to wield weapon
R to read scroll
> to descend
G to pick up item
ARROWS to move

If you can't beat the game, press C
 
Q to quit anytime.
